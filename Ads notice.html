Interest-Based Ads notice.


At Electronic Shop, we use interest-based advertising to show you ads that are more relevant to you. We do this by collecting information about your interests, such as the pages you visit on our website, the products you browse, and the purchases you make. We also collect information from third parties, such as social media platforms and advertising networks.

We use this information to create a profile of your interests, which we then use to show you ads that are more likely to be of interest to you. For example, if you have been browsing laptops on our website, you may see ads for laptops from other retailers.

You can choose to opt out of interest-based advertising at any time. To do this, please visit your ad preferences page.

Here are some of the ways we collect information about your interests:

  • Cookies: Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer or device when you visit our website. We use cookies to collect information about your browsing habits and interests.
  • Web beacons: Web beacons are small pieces of code that are embedded in our website and in emails that we send you. Web beacons collect information about your activity on our website and in emails, such as the pages you visit and the links you click.
  • Third-party data: We also collect information about your interests from third parties, such as social media platforms and advertising networks.
    • Here are some of the ways we use your information to show you interest-based ads:

  • On our website: use your information to show you ads that are more relevant to you on our website.
  • On other websites and apps: We also work with third-party advertising networks to show you interest-based ads on other websites and apps.
  • In emails: We use your information to send you emails that are more relevant to you.

    • We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. We only share your information with advertisers in a way that does not identify you personally.

    You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time. To do this, please visit your ad preferences page.
