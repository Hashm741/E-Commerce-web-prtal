Frequently Asked Questions:


  • What payment methods do you accept?
    • Ans: We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also accept PayPal and Amazon Pay.

  • What are your shipping rates?
    • Ans: Our shipping rates are calculated based on the weight and destination of your order. You can see the estimated shipping costs for your order before you checkout.

  • What is your return policy?
    • Ans: We accept returns for unused and undamaged items within 30 days of purchase. You can find more information about our return policy here.

  • How do I track my order?
    • Ans: You will receive an email with tracking information once your order has shipped. You can also track your order status on our website.

  • What if I have a problem with my order?
    • Ans: If you have any problems with your order, please contact us immediately and we will be happy to help.

    Product Questions:

  • What size should I get?
    • Ans: We have a size chart available on our website to help you find the right size for you. You can also contact us if you need help with sizing.

  • Is this product in stock?
    •  Ans: You can check the availability of a product on its product page. If it is not in stock, you can sign up for a back-in-stock notification.

    Ordering Questions:


  • How do I create an account?
    • A: You can create an account by clicking on the "Create Account" link at the top of any page on our website.

  • How do I place an order?
    • Ans: To place an order, simply add the items you want to your cart and then proceed to checkout. You will be asked to enter your shipping and billing information, and then you will be able to select a payment method and complete your order.

  • Can I cancel my order?
    • Ans: You can cancel your order as long as it has not yet been shipped. To cancel your order, please contact us immediately.

    Shipping Questions:


  • How long will it take for my order to ship?

  • Where do you ship to?

  • What if my order is damaged during shipping?
    • Ans: If your order is damaged during shipping, please contact us immediately and we will send you a replacement.
